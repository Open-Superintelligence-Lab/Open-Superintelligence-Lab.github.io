## Publishing & content

### Auto post-creation pipeline  — status: idea (not now)
Turn a finished research result into a ready-to-ship post with zero manual
formatting.
- Take a result (tutorial md / paper) → draft the X post + Skool post in Vuk's
  voice automatically (already have skills for this).
- Track shipped state by folder: results live in `posted/` once shipped; the
  orchestrator never re-proposes anything already in `posted/`.
- One-click "show me" — open the post/PDF/HTML on screen in Chrome, no
  searching.
- Stretch: stage drafts somewhere Vuk approves with a yes/no before anything
  goes public.

## Distributed contribution (the 4 ways from the mission)

### 1. Token-processing queue  — status: idea
`git clone` / `npm install` → it auto-pulls AI tasks (research, review,
hypothesis writing, coding) and runs them massively in parallel. Contributors
donate LLM tokens to the lab's task queue.

### 2. Personal research space  — status: idea
A private per-user research area (separate from public goals) with prompts,
instructions, and tools for automated AI research + paper writing. May share/
correlate with the public area.

### 3. Human idea-monitoring / approval  — status: idea
A clear way for humans to read the orchestrator's vision and approve / request
changes / reject — so the lab's direction stays legible and accountable.

### 4. Compute contribution  — status: idea
Clone/install something that runs the experiment/research/training queue on a
contributor's own hardware and reports results back.
