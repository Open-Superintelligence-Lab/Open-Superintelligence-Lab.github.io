### Pure Science & Long Term, Open Contribution

Our focus is strictly highest impact onto humanity - which is long-term science and fundamental research. We are not building products to sell. This means that we will probably not have resources to pay salaries. Everything we do is **Open Source** and **Open Research**. We believe that by sharing our breakthroughs, code, and methodologies freely, we can accelerate progress for the entire world at the sacrifice of profit.
