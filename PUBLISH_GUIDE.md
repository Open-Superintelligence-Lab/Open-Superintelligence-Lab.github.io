# Publish Research at Open Superintelligence Lab

Publishing blogs, articles and papers is the only way I know of that will make you a competent researcher.

### Ideas
- Explain high-level concepts or intuition
- Detail the mathematical foundations and proofs
- Implement the logic in PyTorch or Triton
- Build data pipelines and synthetic environments
- Run experiments and analyze the results
- Review and improve existing work

### Ready for Review?
Once you have contributed, you can [request a technical review](/request-review).
