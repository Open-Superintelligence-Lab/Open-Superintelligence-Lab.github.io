# Publish Research at Open Superintelligence Lab

---

## Ideas For Contributing (choose one or more)
- **Explain the Paper**: Translate high-level concepts and intuition into clear, narrative explanations.
- **Background Math**: Deep-dive into the formal mathematical foundations and proofs.
- **Implement Code**: Write clean, modular PyTorch or Triton code for the research logic.
- **Training Dataset**: Build the data pipelines and synthetic environments needed for experiments.
- **Empirical Analysis**: Run experiments, collect insights, and visualize the results.
- **Peer Review**: Collaborate on existing work and provide technical feedback.

---

## Ready for Review?
Once you have contributed, you can [request a technical review](/request-review) to receive feedback and scoring based on our criteria.
