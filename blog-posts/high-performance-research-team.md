### How We Work
- Unlike the broader community, this core team is focused and operates like a standard research team.
- Every project is evaluated against our ultimate goal: maximum long term acceleration of AI research and all science and technology.
- All research, breakthroughs, and even failures are documented openly to accelerate the field.

### Pure Science & Long Term, Open Contribution
Our focus is strictly highest impact onto humanity - which is long-term science and fundamental research and engineering. We are not building products to sell. This means that we will probably not have resources to pay salaries. Everything we do is **Open Source** and **Open Research**. We believe that by sharing our breakthroughs, code, and methodologies freely, we can accelerate progress for the entire world.


### Joining the Team
While we have general community chats on Discord and Douyin, a handpicked team will be built in order to allocate our resources better and separate casual chatting channels from focused research channels.

Anyone is welcome to join, no credentials necessary. Please message me and introduce yourself. More updates on how to get accepted are coming soon (we mainly want to pick serious people who share the same goal and will do the work).
