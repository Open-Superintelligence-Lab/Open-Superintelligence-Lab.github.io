Open Superintelligence Lab is building a core group of high-performing researchers and engineers. 


### Pure Science & Open Contribution
Our focus is strictly highest impact onto humanity - which is long-term science and fundamental research. We are not building products to sell. This means that we will probably not have resources to pay salaries. Everything we do is **Open Source** and **Open Research**. We believe that by sharing our breakthroughs, code, and methodologies freely, we can accelerate progress for the entire world.

### How We Work
- Unlike the broader community direction, this core team posts updates on a daily to weekly basis.
- Every project is evaluated against our ultimate goal: maximum long term acceleration of AI research, all science and engineering.
- All research, breakthroughs, and even failures are documented openly to accelerate the field.


### Joining the Team
While we have general community chats on Discord and Douyin, a handpicked team will be built in order to allocate our resources better and separate casual chatting channels from research channels.

Anyone is welcome to join, no credentials necessary. Please message me and intriduce yourself.
